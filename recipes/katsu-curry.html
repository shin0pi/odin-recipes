Katsu Curry

Easy to cook japanese Katsu Curry!

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Chickenbreast or Vegan chicken
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Broccoli
  • Golden Curry 92g
  • Vegetable fond

Preparation:

  1. wash and cut vegetables
  2. cook rice and fry chicken or vegan thing
  3. cook vegetable in a pot with vegetable fond
  4. after 15 minutes add Golden Curry
  5. Cook till looking good
  6. add in a bowl with cooked rice
  7. top with chicken/vegan and onion
